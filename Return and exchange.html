Return and Exchange Policy

We aim to make your shopping experience with Flipkart seamless and enjoyable. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, we are here to help. Read our return and exchange policy below.

Return Policy

If you are not fully satisfied with a product, you can return it within 30 days of purchase. Ensure the item is in its original condition, with all tags and packaging intact.

  • Items must be unused and in original condition.
  • Items like perishables, personal care, and electronics have specific return guidelines.
  • Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days after the return is received.

Exchange Policy

If you wish to exchange an item, please initiate the exchange within 7 days of delivery.

  • Exchanges are subject to product availability.
  • Items must be in original condition with all tags and packaging intact.
  • Exchanges are available for size, color, or variant-related issues.

Need Further Assistance?

For any further assistance with returns or exchanges, contact our support team.
